Duck Bone Soup

{% endblock %} {% block ingredients_block%} 400g duck
100g bamboo shoots
100g mushrooms
10g ginger (sliced)
6 cups of water
2 tbsp salt
0.5 tbsp white pepper
0.5 tbsp sesame oil
{% endblock %} {% block howToCook_block %} ( 1 ) cut up the duck, boil with cold water.
( 2 ) add sliced ginger.
( 3 ) take out the duck when water boiled.
( 4 ) put duck and bamboo shoots, mushrooms together in a pot, add 6 cups of water and boil.
( 5 ) add prepared salt, white pepper and sesame oil when soup boiled.
