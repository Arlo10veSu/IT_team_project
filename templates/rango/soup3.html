White Gourd and Pork Ribs Soup

{% endblock %} {% block ingredients_block%} 400g pork ribs
200g white gourd
6 cups of water
10g ginger (sliced)
10g green onion
5g sichuan pepper
2 tbsp salt
1 tbsp pepper
1 tbsp vinegar
{% endblock %} {% block howToCook_block %} ( 1 ) cut up the ribs, boil with cold water.
( 2 ) add sliced ginger and sichuan pepper.
( 3 ) take out the ribs when water boiled.
( 4 ) put ribs and white gourd together in a pot, add 6 cups of water and boil.
( 5 ) add prepared salt, pepper and vinegar when soup boiled.
( 6 ) switch to small fire and born half an hour, put in the green onion, then boil 1 hour.
