Duck Bone Soup

{% endblock %} {% block ingredients_block%} 350g black rice 50g glutinous rice 30g corn oil 30g milk 3 eggs 50g sugar {% endblock %} {% block howToCook_block %} ( 1 ) mix the black rice and glutinous rice, put them into a blender and crush.
( 2 ) stir the eggs, then put the eggs and sugar with crushed rice, mix them up.
( 3 ) add prepared corn oil and milk, put on the plastic wrap and cool down the mixture in the refrigerator.
( 4 ) 30 minutes later, steam the mixture for about 40 minutes.
